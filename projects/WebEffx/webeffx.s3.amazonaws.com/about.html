The Gold-Crypto Revolution

Over centuries, Gold has been recognized as a global asset for financial institutions. As a self-sustained ecosystem, Cryptvise is backed by this premium asset. To harness the power of gold with crypto, Cryptvise has partnered with Encom Gold- an international mining company in Tanzania. We also source the highest quality gold from our mines for our gold- backed stablecoin ViseGold. This combines the security of crypto with the value of gold, so if you hold ViseGold in your wallet, it is equal to holding as much gold in your vaults.
